How to Use This System

Quick Start Guide

  • 1 Select a student ID from the dropdown menu
  • 2 Review the student's assessment data and warnings
  • 3 Choose a sample query or write your own custom query
  • 4 Click "Analyze Performance" to generate insights
  • 5 Download your analysis report for future reference

Example Queries

  • What are my three strengths in clinical reasoning?
    Strengths Analysis
  • What areas can I improve on based on feedback?
    Improvement Focus
  • How has my performance changed over time?
    Temporal Analysis
  • What feedback did I receive from attending physicians?
    Evaluator Feedback
  • How am I performing on communication skills?
    Skill Assessment
Pro Tip: For best results with temporal analysis queries (asking about changes "over time"), ensure the selected student has at least 8 assessments. Students with fewer assessments may be off-cycle or MD/PhD students - try general performance queries instead.
